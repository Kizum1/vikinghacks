A hackathon is a social coding event in which teams of developers create projects in a limited amount of time to solve a problem, and compete for prizes!

We welcome all DVC students interested in design, programming and problem-solving. No prior coding experience is required to participate!

Diablo Valley College Cafeteria - 321 Golf Club Rd, Pleasant Hill

Visit the “Register” page on our website to RSVP for the event. You MUST RSVP in order to participate in this hackathon.

Finding teams before the hackathon is allowed and highly encouraged

Don’t fret! We will have team-building time during the hackathon during which you can find like-minded students to build a project with.
