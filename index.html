Viking Hacks 2023!

April 28th - 29th @ Diablo Valley College

VikingHacks is a 2 day in-person hackathon event hosted by Google Developers Student Club at Diablo Valley College.

It is a great opportunity for students to learn new skills, build projects, and network with other students.

This year, we are hosting our 3rd annual hackathon. We are excited to see what you will create!

Viking Hacks 2023 Theme: Sustainability

What is Viking Hacks

A Hackathon that provides a platform for creative minds to imagine and develop fresh solutions for the greater good.

When is it?

Friday April 28 to Saturday April 29th in the DVC Cafeteria
