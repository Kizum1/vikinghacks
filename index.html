Viking Hacks is back

April 28th - 30th @ Diablo Valley College

VikingHacks is a 2 day event hosted by Google Developers Student Club at Diablo Valley College.

It is a great opportunity for students to learn new skills, build projects, and network with other students.

This year, we are hosting our 2nd in-person hackathon. We are excited to see what you will create!
